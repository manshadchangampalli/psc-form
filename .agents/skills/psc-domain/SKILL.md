# PSC Domain Skill

This skill provides domain knowledge about the Kerala Public Service Commission (PSC) ecosystem to ensure the landing page resonates with aspirants.

## Target Audience
- Aspirants aged 18-35 preparing for various state government jobs.
- High competition levels (LDC, VEO, CPO, etc.).
- Users are often looking for daily consistency and easy access to study materials (like WhatsApp updates).

## Key Selling Points
- **WhatsApp Integration**: High engagement tool in Kerala.
- **Daily 10 Questions**: Manageable and "trending" (updated regularly).
- **Urgency**: "First 100 students" and "3-month free trial".

## Tone and Style
- Motivational yet professional.
- Trustworthy and helpful.
- Direct call to action (CTA).
